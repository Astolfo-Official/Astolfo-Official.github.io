Mini-Biography

Xinwei Ji (纪鑫炜) is a second-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Physics and Astronomy at the University of Delaware, specializing in chemical physics. He is currently working on his doctoral research under the supervision of Tao E. Li.

His research interests lie at the intersection of light-matter interaction, electronic structure theory, molecular simulation, and AI-enhanced scientific research. He develops and applies open-source simulation tools for self-consistent light-matter dynamics.

Before starting his Ph.D., Xinwei received his B.S. in Chemistry from Xiamen University, where he built a background in quantum chemistry. He is happy to discuss research ideas and potential collaboration opportunities; prospective group members can also visit the TEL Research Group website.
